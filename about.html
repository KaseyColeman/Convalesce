About Me

I created this website because there are many resources available for mental health but many of them come at a steep cost. A large population of mental health sufferers and cannot afford to spend money on these type of things due to trict budgets or the price is too high to use long term. Convalesce is different. All our resources are free and available to anyone who makes an account.It also includes many resources and hotlines that are also available for free. Mental health should not solely depend on if you can afford expensive memberships and subscriptions. I hope that in creating this website it will give users access to things that may help them.
