About Me

I created this website because there are many resources available for mental health but many of them come at a steep cost. For so many of us for whatever reason cannot afford to spend money on these try of things as we may have strict budgets or there is just too high of prices to use long term. Convalesce is different, we have all our resources for free and it is available to anyone who makes an account. This will also contain many resources and hotlines that will also be available for free. Mental health should not just depend on if you can afford expensive memberships and subscriptions. I hope that in creating this website it will give many accesses to things that may help them in some way.
