Why Choose Convalesce

We Care

we understand what its like to sturggle with mental illness and not have many options to turn to. We are a group that is here to help. We are here to help you throught these emotional times.

We Understand many layers to mental illness

Not every mental health resource will work for every person. Thats why Convalesce has a varity of different things that can help such as journaling, sliding emotional charts and the ability to have your healthcare worker added directly to your profile.

This is a free and open to use resource

Many resources are expensive and come at a high cost. We understand that this is not an option for so many people as they dont have disposible income for subscription fees. We are a completely free resource that is availiable to anyone that would like to create an account.
